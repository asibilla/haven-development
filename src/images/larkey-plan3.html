About this property

Brand NEW construction with breathtaking views! This home is a MUST SEE!! Upon entrance into 286 Via Cima Ct you can't help but notice the 10' 6" tall entry-way giving you the grand entrance feel in this beautiful new single story construction. This home offers 4 spacious bedrooms, 3 full bathrooms and separate powder room for guests.  The open kitchen/great room with raised 9' ceilings offers a large space for entertaining.  This new home sits on 14,400 sq feet of flat lot with no rear neighbors, complete privacy and breathtaking views of Mt. Diablo and the San Ramon Valley.  Some of the many high-end finishes include maple cabinets, hickory hardwood floors, Calcutta trento quartz slab counters throughout, and 16' California doors looking out to your private lot and outdoor shower!!
